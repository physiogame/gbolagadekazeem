About

Science, learning and software in one research practice.

Gbolagade Kazeem Adewale is a physiologist, neuroscience researcher, medical education innovator and research software developer. He earned a BSc in Physiology with Second Class Upper Honours from the University of Medical Sciences, Ondo, where he achieved distinctions in Neuroanatomy, Neurophysiology I and Neurophysiology II.

He is currently pursuing a full-time MSc in Physiology at the University of Ilorin, with a focus on neurophysiology. His work brings medical science together with serious-game design, interactive assessment and software development.

His long-term goal is to develop and evaluate accessible interactive systems that improve scientific reasoning, knowledge retention, learner confidence and conceptual understanding in medical and health-science education, including in resource-limited settings.
